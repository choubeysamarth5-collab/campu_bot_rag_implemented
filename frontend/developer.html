FAQ Training (CSV)

Bulk-add FAQs to strengthen the fallback that answers questions when both Gemini and Groq fail. CSV columns (header row required): 

category,keywords,answer_en,answer_hi,answer_mr,answer_ta,answer_te

• category must be one of: fees, admissions, exams, hostel, library, placements, scholarships, timetable, other
• keywords: separate multiple keywords with a semicolon, e.g. fee;payment;due date
• answer_en is required; other language columns are optional
• wrap any field containing a comma in double quotes
